Digital Certificates

Identity Verification

Digital certificates are used to verify the identity of individuals, organizations, or devices. They can be used for a variety of purposes, such as authenticating users for online services or securing IoT devices.

Encryption Keys

Digital certificates also include encryption keys, which are used to encrypt and decrypt data. By using digital certificates, organizations can ensure that only authorized parties have access to sensitive information.

Revocation

Digital certificates can be revoked if they are compromised or no longer needed. This ensures that a compromised certificate cannot be used to impersonate an authorized party.

Certificate Transparency

Certificate transparency is a system that logs all SSL/TLS certificates issued by certificate authorities. This helps prevent the issuance of fraudulent certificates and provides greater transparency into the certificate ecosystem.
