SSL/TLS Certificates

Secure Communication

SSL/TLS certificates enable secure communication between a website or application and its users. By encrypting data in transit, SSL/TLS certificates protect sensitive information from interception by attackers.

Authentication

SSL/TLS certificates also provide authentication, ensuring that users are communicating with the intended website or application and not an imposter. This helps prevent phishing and other types of attacks.

SEO

SSL/TLS certificates can also improve a website's search engine ranking. Google and other search engines give preference to sites that use SSL/TLS encryption, as it is seen as a signal of trustworthiness.

Risk Management Framework

SSL/TLS certificates also provide trust indicators, such as the padlock icon in the browser address bar. These indicators reassure users that their communication with the website or application is secure and trustworthy.
