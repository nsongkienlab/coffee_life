Compliance Audits

HIPAA

The Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) is a federal law that requires healthcare organizations to implement security measures to protect patients' personal and medical information. HIPAA compliance audits evaluate healthcare organizations' compliance with these requirements.

PCI-DSS

The Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI-DSS) is a set of security standards that govern the processing, storage, and transmission of credit card data. PCI-DSS compliance audits evaluate organizations' compliance with these standards.

SOX

The Sarbanes-Oxley Act (SOX) is a federal law that requires public companies to establish and maintain internal controls over financial reporting. SOX compliance audits evaluate companies' compliance with these requirements.

GDPR

The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) is a European Union regulation that governs the processing of personal data. GDPR compliance audits evaluate organizations' compliance with these requirements.
