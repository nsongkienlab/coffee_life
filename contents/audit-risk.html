Risk Assessments

Threat Assessment

A threat assessment is a type of risk assessment that identifies potential threats to an organization's IT systems and processes. Threat assessments can help organizations prioritize security measures and allocate resources more effectively.

Asset Inventory

An asset inventory is a type of risk assessment that identifies all of the IT assets owned by an organization, including hardware, software, and data. Asset inventories can help organizations identify and address vulnerabilities in their IT systems.

Business Impact Analysis

A business impact analysis is a type of risk assessment that evaluates the potential impact of security incidents on an organization's operations and finances. Business impact analyses can help organizations prioritize security measures based on their potential impact.

Risk Management Framework

A risk management framework is a structured approach to identifying, assessing, and managing risks. Risk management frameworks can help organizations establish a comprehensive risk management program that integrates risk assessment, risk mitigation, and risk monitoring.
